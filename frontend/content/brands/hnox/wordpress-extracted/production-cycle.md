It is important to check that the material supplied is indeed of the required quality.
The incoming controls involve randomly checking the steel coils or castings with a chemical reagent that highlights the presence of molybdenum on the tested surface, a chemical element that is essential for defining the quality of stainless steel, which in our case is AISI 316.
